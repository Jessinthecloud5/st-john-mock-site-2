About Us

We exist to glorify God by knowing Him, growing together, and serving others in our community and beyond.

Our Church

St. John Presbyterian Church is a Christ-centered community committed to worshiping God, growing in His Word, and serving others with His love.

For decades, we have been a place where people from all walks of life can find hope, build meaningful relationships, and deepen their faith in Jesus Christ.

Whether you are exploring faith or looking for a church home, we are glad you're here and invite you to become a part of our story.

Our Beliefs

We are a Bible-believing church that holds to the historic Christian faith as summarized in our denominational confessions. Our beliefs shape how we worship, grow, and serve together.

Our beliefs are rooted in Scripture and guide everything we do as a church family.
We believe the Bible is the written Word of God, inspired by the Holy Spirit, and is the infallible and trustworthy authority for faith and life.
We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, who are co-equal in power and glory.
We believe that Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God, who became man, lived a sinless life, died on the cross to save us from sin, rose bodily from the dead, and will return in glory.
We believe the Holy Spirit is present in the world to make people aware of their need for Jesus Christ, to live within every believer from the moment of salvation, and to guide, equip, and empower them for service.
We believe salvation is a free gift from God, received through faith alone in Jesus Christ, whose death and resurrection purchased our forgiveness and reconciliation with God.
We believe the church is the spiritual body of Christ, made up of all believers throughout history, called to worship God, grow in faith, and carry out Christ's mission in the world.
We believe in the bodily resurrection of the dead, the eternal life and joy of believers in the presence of God, and the renewal of all creation.
We live out our faith by loving God with all our heart, soul, mind, and strength, and by loving our neighbors as ourselves through service, compassion, and justice.
