Serving Beyond Our Church Walls

At St. John Presbyterian Church, missions are an expression of our faith in action. Through partnerships and outreach programs, we support education, healthcare, housing, and community assistance for those who need it most.

JUDEO CHRISTIAN HEALTH CLINIC (JCHC)

Providing free, quality healthcare services to medically underserved residents in our community.

The clinic offers compassionate care while supporting the physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being of individuals and families.

THE LEARNING CENTER

Providing educational opportunities and scholarship assistance for children and families in need.

The Learning Center helps students develop academically while creating a safe and nurturing environment for growth.

MCCLAIN FOUNDATION HOME

A family-oriented residential home supporting women with developmental disabilities.

For decades, the McClain Foundation Home has helped residents achieve greater independence while living in a caring and supportive environment.

THRIFT SHOP

Providing affordable clothing and household goods while supporting community outreach efforts.

Every purchase and donation helps fund ministry programs and assists local families in need.

FAITH IN ACTION

Every ministry we support reflects our commitment to sharing God's love through service, compassion, and community engagement.
