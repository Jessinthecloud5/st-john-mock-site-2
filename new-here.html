Frequently Asked Questions

You can expect a warm welcome, meaningful worship, Biblical teaching, and a caring church family. Whether you're visiting alone or with family, we're glad you're here and hope you'll feel at home.
Worship services are held every Sunday morning at 11:30 AM in the sanctuary. We also offer Sunday School classes for all ages beginning at 10:30 AM, and the Sunshine Class at 9:45 AM.
Our church is located in Tampa, Florida at 4120 N. MacDill Ave., Tampa, FL 33607. We are conveniently situated in the heart of Tampa, easily accessible from I-275 or Dale Mabry Highway.
Come as you are! You'll see a wide range of attire at our Sunday services, from casual jeans and t-shirts to business casual and traditional Sunday clothes. We care about you, not what you're wearing.
Yes! We offer childcare for infants and toddlers during the 11:30 AM service, as well as Children's Sunday School classes for elementary-aged kids led by Emily Davis at 10:30 AM.
We are a Christ-centered, Bible-believing congregation in the Presbyterian tradition. We believe that Scripture is the final authority in faith and life, and we are committed to sharing Christ's grace. You can read more under the "Our Beliefs" section on our About page.
There are many ways to connect: joining a weekly Bible study, serving in local missions (like our Health Clinic or Thrift Shop), singing in the choir, or helping out with children's events. Feel free to speak with any of the pastors or complete our contact form to learn more!
We actively support local ministries including the Judeo Christian Health Clinic, McClain Foundation Home, The Learning Center, and our own church Thrift Shop. Our Missions page provides details on how we partner with each organization.
We host periodic "Inquirers Classes" for anyone interested in learning more about membership. Attending is a great way to meet leaders and find out what membership involves without any obligation. Contact Pastor Jane Smith for scheduling.
You can reach our administrative office during open hours at (813) 555-0199 or email us anytime at info@stjohnpres.com. We look forward to answering any questions you may have!
